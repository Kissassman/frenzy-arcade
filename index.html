About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced survival game where you must outlast endless waves of monsters. Collect upgrades, master your movement, and climb the leaderboard! Enjoy a seamless, iOS-inspired experience on both mobile and desktop.

  • Genre: Action, Survival
  • Platform: Browser (Mobile & PC)

How to Play

  1. Use your touchscreen or keyboard (WASD/Arrow keys) to move. On mobile, tap the screen edges to control movement.
  2. Your hero attacks automatically – focus on dodging and collecting upgrades.
  3. Survive as long as possible to set a new high score!
